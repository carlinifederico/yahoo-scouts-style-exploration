Delivery 01 !

First Exploration

A wide net across many styles.

Client Feedback !

Make the mascots simpler and legible at small sizes — less cartoonish and childish. The client leaned toward the clean light-blue & purple shapes as the clearest, most scalable direction to build on.

Delivery 02 !

Second Exploration

Comic energy vs. cleaner editorial & soft 3D.

Client Feedback !

Go more editorial, less comic-book (think Notion). Keep it modern & sleek like Apple while holding the Yahoo wink. Lean into Memoji / Duolingo simplicity — a few still read a little too juvenile.

Delivery 03 !

Third Exploration

Flat, high-contrast illustrations — "beautiful explorations."

Client Feedback !

These landed as bold, high-contrast illustrations with limited palettes — clever, never generic. Avoid heavy gradients and Memphis clichés. The #5, #10 and #15 directions felt the most ownable.
